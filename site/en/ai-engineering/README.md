# AI Engineering

AI Engineering describes the methodology layer of AI-Enhanced Software Engineering: what changes when AI becomes part of software delivery, what does not change, and how teams organize specification, harness, verification, and knowledge reuse.

## Method Frame

The Chinese foundation articles define the method layer before OpenSpec, Compound, and AISEE:

- traditional software engineering vs. AI-enhanced software engineering
- what AI does not change
- the engineer's responsibilities in the AI era
- Prompt to Spec
- Tool to Harness
- experience to compounding
